The Tribunal

Autonomous Content System Architecture
Prepared by Soumik · 2026

Ⅰ. Executive Overview

The Goal: A fully automated marketing ecosystem that runs daily without requiring active management.

The Tribunal system scans the internet for trending MENA conversations, generates original content using real vote data, publishes across 9 platforms simultaneously, tracks performance, and dynamically rewrites its own strategy every week based on hard data.

Required Weekly Management: Under 30 minutes.

Ⅱ. The Daily Operational Loop

Every single day, the system executes the following sequence automatically:

Time (Gulf) Phase Action
10:00 PM SCAN Apify scrapes Reddit, X trending hashtags, LinkedIn, and YouTube. Perplexity AI pulls the top 5 live MENA news stories. All 730 database content pieces are ranked by current relevance.
6:00 AM GENERATE Claude analyzes trend data alongside historical performance. It authors a LinkedIn post, X thread, short video script, and image card using real vote results. No generic filler.
7:00 AM ONE TAP A Telegram message is sent to Kareem with yesterday's vote count, top debate, and today's content. Takes < 3 mins to approve. Fallback: Auto-posts highest-scoring variant after 4 hours.
9AM - 9PM PUBLISH Blotato distributes approved content to all 9 platforms at optimal MENA time windows.
All Day REACT Reddit monitor scans twice daily to draft automated, data-backed replies. Spike detector checks hourly—if a debate hits 5x volume, follow-up content is generated and an alert is fired.
Sunday LEARN The self-improvement engine analyzes the week's performance, identifies winning formats/times, and rewrites content weights for the upcoming week.

Ⅲ. The Content Engine

The system transforms raw data into platform-native formats.

  • Video (30–45s): Faceless AI video generated via Blotato (AI imagery + ElevenLabs voiceover). Script format: Hook Stat → Debate Question → Result Reveal. Pushed to TikTok, IG Reels, YouTube Shorts.
  • Image Cards: Crimson split-cards showcasing real vote percentages. Auto-generated via template in seconds. Pushed to Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, Pinterest.
  • LinkedIn Posts: Data-led, 150–200 words. Opens with vote numbers, details MENA country breakdowns, and ends with a high-conversion question.
  • X Threads: 4–6 tweets. Hook stat → Data breakdown → Surprising finding → Link to The Tribunal.
  • Newsletter: Weekly auto-drafted Beehiiv email featuring the top 3 debates, a prediction update, and a Voice spotlight. Awaits Kareem's final review.

Ⅳ. The Self-Learning Mechanism

By week 8, the system will understand The Tribunal's audience better than any manual team.

  • READS: Ingests a full week of data (votes per post, platform, and category).
  • SCORES: Identifies over-indexing categories (e.g., gender debates at 3.1x average) and winning formats.
  • UPDATES: Rewrites the PostgreSQL/database content weights. Prioritizes high-performing categories and winning formats.
  • REPORTS: Pushes a highly readable, 5-line Telegram summary to Kareem detailing what worked, what failed, and 3 specific automated adjustments made.

Ⅵ. Delivery Acceptance Checklist

All items are pass/fail and testable on handover day.

Data & Sourcing

  • Supabase trends table updates daily with tagged sources.
  • 5 random content queue posts exactly match database debate IDs and vote counts.
  • Notion trend pairings share clear themes (no random mismatching).
  • news_feed table logs 5 MENA-specific headlines daily.

Content Quality

  • Zero invented statistics; numbers match the DB perfectly.
  • 4 out of 5 posts reference specific MENA contexts/countries.
  • last_posted_at logic works; no debates repeated within 14 days.
  • Video scripts strictly adhere to 65–100 word limits.

Distribution

  • Approved test posts appear on all 9 platforms within 2 hours.
  • scheduled_at times fall strictly within 8–10 AM or 7–9 PM Gulf Time.
  • Disconnecting a platform triggers a Telegram error alert within 30 mins.

Self-Learning & Outreach

  • content_weights table values shift after a Sunday run.
  • Sunday 5-line Telegram report successfully delivers.
  • Instantly.ai sequences automatically pause upon reply.
  • voices_outreach database grows by ~10 contacts every weekday.

User Experience (Kareem)

  • Daily digest hits Telegram between 7:00 and 7:15 AM Gulf Time.
  • Notion dashboard "Last Updated" timestamp is never older than 6 hours.
  • Full approval flow takes Kareem < 5 minutes.
  • Manual database vote spikes trigger Telegram alerts within 10 minutes.
